This site is currently under construction.

Rusty Palette is an overlay for Rust intended to offer a variety of Quality of Life improvements as well as tactical advantages while steering clear of anything that could be called 'hacking.' The first feature that will exist is a painting feature which will blow Rustangelo out of the water in terms of both speed and quality. Using some fairly specialized algorithms and a hidden colour palette accessible via pixel-perfect color selection, I can create paintings with more detail in under 5 minutes.

Upcoming features include death tracker, alerts for players of interest coming online or going offline, a valuable loot highlighter to help with raids, the ability to make markings on your map, and guitar macros.

Beta release will hopefully be in Mid-december, and full release in early January. Cost of the app will be 5 dollars.
