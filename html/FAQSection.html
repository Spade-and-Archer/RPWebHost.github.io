Is use of Rusty Palette considered hacking?
No. I do not know of anyone who as been banned for use of my software and Rusty palette doesn't do anything Facepunch considers hacking. We believe that hackers take the fun out of Rust and we do not support any malicious software designed to give you an unfair advantage. If Facepunch does object to any functionality of this software, that feature will be removed without warning from all versions of Rusty Palette.

Will running RP affect my game's performance?
No. Rusty Palette should run seamlessly alongside your game, even on budget gaming rigs. When active in the background RP uses ~1% of a single CPU core and about 0.2 GB of RAM.

What happens if this project isn't profitable?
If this project completely flops and I'm convinced that there is no way it will take off, then I will publish the source code on github, remove the Digital Rights Management, and publish it as a new version. Everyone will have full access for free.
Is my information secure with you?
Yes. We use PayPal so none of your payment information is ever stored and your email will only ever be used for password reset. Your steamID (which we use for verification) is hashed and salted before being transmitted, and your password is hashed twice--Once before being transmitted, and again with a unique salt when it is stored. We know Rust players are a salty and tech-savy bunch, so we've ensured our database is iron-clad.
